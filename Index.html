Our Story

Where bold flavours
meet the open sky.

Perched on the 3rd floor of Hotel Gulbahar at Powai Naka, The Urban Grill Kitchen is Satara's most celebrated rooftop destination — a space born from a love for exceptional food and the magic of dining under the stars.

From sizzling tandoor starters and rich Indian curries to wood-fired pizzas, Asian fusion, and craft mocktails — every dish is a celebration of flavour.
