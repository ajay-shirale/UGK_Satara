Our Story

Real Food.
Real Views.
Real Satara.

Perched on the 3rd floor of Hotel Gulbahar at Powai Naka, The Urban Grill Kitchen is Satara's most talked-about rooftop destination. Built around a love for exceptional food and the magic of dining under the open sky.

From live tandoor starters and rich Indian curries to wood-fired pizzas, Asian fusion, and craft mocktails — every dish is made with intention.
